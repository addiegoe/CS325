Welcome to Mastermind

Play against the program! The program will choose four colored pegs and place them in order. The possible colors are: red, blue, yellow, green, orange, and purple. No duplicate colors are allowed. You get 8 guesses to solve the puzzle. At each step, the program will give you a clue. If the program places a white peg, you've guessed a color correctly. If the program places a black peg, you've guessed the color and position of a peg correctly. The clues are not in order of the peg placement. Good luck!
